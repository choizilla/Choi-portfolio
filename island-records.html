About

Celebrating their 60th anniversary, legendary label Island records first debuted their own range in the form of a clothing range in 2018 via everpress. Witnessing the popularity and growth of e-Commerce in that area, they commisoned this large scale department store to host all Island musicians and artists. This store is styled to compliment the styling of their official website.
