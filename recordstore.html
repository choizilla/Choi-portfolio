Record Store

Redesigning an online vinyl record shop founded in 1998.

www.recordstore.co.uk

Founded in 1998, Record Store boasts to be "the best record emporium on the good ol' web". They emerged as an oasis for music fans unable to find their latest fix on the highstreet, specialising in the best new bands and the cream of established alternative artists, becoming one of the leading online music retailers in the UK.

Brief

As the online store has not been loved or up-kept for over 5 years, some areas of the design and features were beginning to look and feel outdated. Usability wise, it serves it purpose in selling music, and does that very well through the product range and the content they curate, especially through their wonder emails and intriguing campaigns. There are two parts to the project--refreshing the brand so that it had more of a cohesive yet stronger character, as well as updating website to compete with other physical music sellers.

  • Refresh the Record Store brand to have a stronger personality
  • Redesign the webstore
