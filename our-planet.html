About

Our Planet is a groundbreaking Netflix original documentary series narrated by Sir David Attenborough, first aired in April 2019. This store was comissioned to promote and sell the soundtrack produced for this series.

Composed by Stephen Price, this album was released on vinyl and CD in an ecological packaging material made from algae and FSC fibres, Shiro Alga Carta, developed as a way to reprocess the damaging algal blooms of the Venice lagoon into a sustainable paper product. The "Our Planet" soundtrack release marks a month of increased awareness about the natural world, culminating in Earth Day on 22 April, 2019.
